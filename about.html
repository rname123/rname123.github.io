How I Opened My Jalebi Shop and Became a Successful Business Owner, Insiders' Guide
The crisp layers of a khurma reflect the intricacies of Indian culture. Soft jalebis are fused with mathris, chawal and iced milk as a favorite food all over. With events like Ganesh Utsav, Diwali and Sankranti (for example) going on, you can even see sweet jalebis in desserts such as milk pedas. No matter how you take it or eat it - memory lane snapshots from lively summers from your childhood makes way for contemplations about life's true meaning and transformations.
